All About Karnataka

Karnataka is known for its tradition and scenic beauty with places like Mysore, Hampi, and Pattadakallu to see and is a beautiful land blessed with timeless monuments, world heritage sites, lush green forests, diverse wildlife, hill stations, stunning beaches, and vibrant cultures. It hosts a total of 30 districts and it's capital is Bangalore or Electronic city.

Some Adventurous Activities

Adventure trip to Coorg with cottage or tent stay

Spend a night at one of the most picturesque campsites in Coorg and stay in colourful cottage rooms or alpine tents. With the backdrop of green mountains and sprawling plantations, you'll try your hand at different activities like quad biking, air rifle shooting, zip-lining and more. You can also go swimming in the nearby lake or relax with views of the distant hills. In the evening, you'll gather around a private bonfire to enjoy a calm night under the stars with some music.

Adventure trip to Coorg with cottage or tent stay

Spend a night in comfortable Swiss tents at a beautiful campsite in Mysore and enjoy a fun holiday with your family or friends. The campsite offers a range of exciting group activities, from outdoor sports like football or cricket to indoor games like carrom or board games. You can also head out to explore local attractions in Mysore like the Brindavan Gardens or the Mysore Palace or simply relax at the campsite and take a dip in the swimming pool. In the evening, enjoy an outdoor movie experience with your fellow campers and end the day around a crackling bonfire.
